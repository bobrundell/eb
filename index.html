eBackgrounds

Welcome to eBackgrounds - a timely and accurate on-line source for your pre-employment and due diligence background investigation needs. eBackgrounds investigations may include, but are not limited to, criminal and civil litigation record checks, traffic record checks, education and employment verifications, personal and professional reference checks, and credit history information. By utilizing eBackgrounds, you can request and retrieve your investigations in electronic format quickly and securely.

eBackgrounds is an extension of services offered by Rehmann Corporate Investigative Services, a professional investigation and security consulting firm headed by former management officials of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI). If you have questions about eBackgrounds, contact Anne McDonald.
